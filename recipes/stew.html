Stew !

Description

This slow cooker beef stew is the perfect low maintenance meal for chilly fall and winter nights. Full of cozy flavor and hearty ingredients, this beef stew recipe will warm you up from the inside out.

Ingredients

  • Stew meat
  • Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Broth
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Place the beef in the slow cooker.
  2. Toss with flour, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add the broth, vegetables, Worcestershire sauce, paprika, garlic, and bay leaf.
  4. Stir to combine.
  5. Cover the Crock-Pot and cook on Low for 8 to 12 hours or on High for 4 to 6 hours.
